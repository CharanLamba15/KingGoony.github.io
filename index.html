Life on alien planet

Living on an a different planet is always my dream. If we humans are able to achieve this, we can not only gain more resources but we maybe can evolve ourselves too. Because the characteristics of this new planet are different, if we stay on this planet for a while our body will naturally adapt to the planet which causes our bodies to evolve.
